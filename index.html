Sundar pichai

full name Pichai Sundararajan.He is the chief executive officer (CEO) of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google

  • 1972: born on july 10 in madurai tamilnadu india.
  • 1980: he grew up in a middle class tamil family at two room appartment in ashok nagar and has one brother
  • 1985: he was a bright and active student.He excelled in his academics. He is a sports enthusiast and was the captain of his school cricket team
  • 1993: he completed his btech at iit kharagpur in metallurgical engeneering
  • 1994: he got scholarship to study at stanford university in material science and semiconductor physics.hus father drained all 1 year savings to send him to america
  • 1998: he planned to persue his phd at stanford but droped out and joined applied materials a sillicon vally semiconductor maker as an engeneer and product manager.
  • 2002: he completed his mba at wharton school of the university. he was appointed as management consultant at mckinsey and company he worked there for 2 years
  • 2004: pichai joined google initially he worked as google search tool bar as a part of small team
  • 2005: he married anjali who he fell in love at his btech and now has 2 children.
  • 2008: pichai convinced the cofounders of google larry page and sergy brin to launch googles own browser .he played a big role in launching google chrome browser. he was promoted to vice president of product development.
